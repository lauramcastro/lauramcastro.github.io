Academic footprint

My research focuses on software testing (automated, model, and property-based testing), applied to software in general, and distributed, concurrent, functional systems in particular. Most of my work takes place inside the BEAM.

You can check my latest publications of particular relevance in the sites below. If you happen to be interested in related areas, I am always open to collaboration!

If your institution or company does not provide access to the websites above, or if you are browsing them from a private computer and you cannot reach the linked publications, do not hesitate to contact me and ask me for a copy.
