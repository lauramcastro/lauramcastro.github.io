Laura M. Castro

I am a professor at Universidade da Coruña (UDC) and a senior researcher at CITIC.
I am the chair of Cátedra CICAS, a joint project between UDC and Corunet to boost Open Science through Software.
I am part of the BEAM community, and member of the Erlang Ecosystem Foundation.
I am a member of CEXEF (Gender and Feminist Studies Centre, UDC).

Research

My research focuses on software testing (automated, model, and property-based testing), applied to software in general, and distributed, concurrent, functional systems in particular.
Most of my work takes place inside the BEAM.

You can check my latest publications of particular relevance in the sites below.
If you happen to be interested in related areas, I am always open to collaboration!

If your institution or company does not provide access to the following websites, or if you are browsing them from a personal device and cannot reach the linked publications, do not hesitate to contact me and ask me for a copy.

Teaching

I am the coordinator of two undegraduate courses offered as part of the Degree in Software Engineering at UDC.

Software Architecture

Compulsory subject for 3rd-year students with a concentration on Software Engineering. Elective subject for 4th-year students with a concentration on Computer Science.

Software Verification and Validation

Compulsory subject for 4th-year students with a concentration on Software Engineering. Elective subject for 4th-year students with a concentration on Computer Science.
